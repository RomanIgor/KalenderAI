Privacy Policy — KalenderAI

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Overview

KalenderAI ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free productivity tool that helps users extract appointment information from text, photos, and voice recordings and add them to their calendar. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed, how, and for what purpose. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

KalenderAI does not require account registration. No personal user profiles are created or stored by us.

4. AI Processing — Third-Party Services

To extract appointment information from your input, we transmit your text, image, or voice data to the following AI service:

  • Groq, Inc. — AI inference service (Groq Privacy Policy). Data is sent over encrypted HTTPS and used solely for real-time inference. Groq does not store or train on user data submitted via API according to their terms of service.

No data is transmitted to any other third party for AI processing.

5. Google Fonts

The app loads fonts (Outfit, JetBrains Mono) from Google Fonts (fonts.google.com). When loading fonts, your IP address is transmitted to Google's servers. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details. Fonts are cached locally after the first load, minimizing subsequent requests.

6. Calendar Integration

When you choose to export an event to Google Calendar or Outlook, you are redirected to that service's own website or a calendar file (.ics) is downloaded to your device. KalenderAI does not have access to your calendar data. Any data shared with Google Calendar or Outlook is governed by those services' own privacy policies.

7. No Tracking, No Advertising

KalenderAI does not use:

  • Analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising identifiers or ad networks
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Cross-app or cross-site tracking
  • Fingerprinting or device identification

8. Microphone and Camera Permissions

The app requests microphone access (for voice input) and camera/gallery access (for photo input) only when you explicitly activate those features. These permissions are used exclusively for the stated purpose of extracting appointment information and are not used for any other purpose.

9. Data Retention

We do not store your text, photo, or audio data on our servers. Data transmitted for AI processing is used in real-time and immediately discarded after the response is returned. The only data retained is an anonymous request counter (no user identifiers) used for service monitoring.

Your preferences (theme, language) are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and remain under your control. You can clear them at any time through your browser or device settings.

10. Children's Privacy

KalenderAI is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through this app, please contact us so we can address it.

11. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — You may request information about data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure — You may request deletion of your data.
  • Right to object — You may object to certain processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability — You may request your data in a portable format.

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, most of these rights are satisfied by the architecture of the app. For any requests or questions, contact us at the address below.

12. Security

All data transmissions between the app and our backend, and between our backend and third-party AI services, are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We do not transmit your data over unencrypted connections.
