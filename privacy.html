Privacy Policy — KalenderAI

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Overview

KalenderAI does not collect, store, or share personal data. No account is required.

2. Data Processing

Text, photos, and voice recordings are sent to Groq API for AI processing only. This data is processed in real-time and is NOT stored on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Groq (groq.com) for AI processing. See their privacy policy at https://groq.com/privacy-policy

4. Anonymous Statistics

We count total daily API requests anonymously — no user data or IP addresses are stored.

5. No Tracking

No analytics, no advertising identifiers, no tracking of any kind.
