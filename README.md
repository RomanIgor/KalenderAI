# 🗓 KalenderAI

> **Add appointments to Google Calendar automatically — via text, photo, or voice.**  
> Powered by Groq AI · Free · No installation needed · Works on any smartphone

---

## ✨ What it does

KalenderAI uses AI to extract dates, times, and locations from anything you throw at it — and sends them straight to Google Calendar with one tap. No more manual typing.

| Input | Example |
|-------|---------|
| ✏️ **Text** | *"Team meeting Thursday May 15 at 2pm in the conference room"* |
| 📷 **Photo** | Photograph an invitation, ticket, flyer, or handwritten note |
| 🎙️ **Voice** | Say the appointment out loud in German or any language |

## 🔧 Setup (2 minutes)

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Go to [console.groq.com](https://console.groq.com) → create a free account
3. Navigate to **API Keys** → **Create API Key**
4. Paste the key (starts with `gsk_...`) into the app → **Save**
5. Done ✅

> **Why Groq?** It's completely free, extremely fast, and works without any credit card. You get 14,400 free requests per day.
